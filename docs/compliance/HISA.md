# HISA-Aligned Readiness Mapping

This page uses HISA-aligned operational categories as a digital modeling and readiness reference for TrackMind Nexus. It does not claim formal HISA certification, HISA approval, regulatory acceptance, legal interpretation, or completed integration with HISA systems.

Formal obligations and determinations remain with the relevant authorities, programs, regulators, racetrack officials, and legal/compliance teams. TrackMind evidence should support review; it should not replace official filings, human authority, or jurisdiction-specific compliance work.

## Operating Categories

- Racetrack safety and operating conditions.
- Surface inspection, maintenance, and condition evidence.
- Equine identity, eligibility, welfare observations, and veterinary review workflows.
- Medication, treatment, and restricted-status evidence where legally appropriate.
- Race-day operations, scratches, starts, stops, official results, and stewarding evidence.
- Incident reporting, emergency response, corrective action, and evidence custody.
- Personnel roles, credentials, training, and authorization.
- Asset readiness for gates, rails, cameras, sensors, emergency equipment, restricted zones, and facilities.
- Audit trails, retention, review packages, ARCI-aligned mapping, and local commission mapping.

| Control objective | System evidence | Owner | Audit artifact |
|---|---|---|---|
| Establish governed, auditable racetrack AI operations. | RBAC, immutable audit logs, approval workflow, cited rulebook answers. | Compliance Officer | Policy exports, audit log hashes, approval records. |
| Protect safety-critical decisions from unapproved automation. | ResponsibleAIGovernor blocks regulated actions until approved. | Responsible AI Governor | Safety-rule test reports and approval records. |

## Operational oversight categories

HISA-aligned readiness evidence is organized under racetrack management, racing office operations, maintenance/facilities, safety, and accreditation. A single universal evidence package can support HISA alongside ISO 42001, ISO 27001, ISO 27701, ISO 31000, SOC 2, PCI DSS, ARCI, and local racing rules when the package includes source, audit, event, Digital Twin, AI recommendation, owner, cadence, and framework-mapping metadata.

This page describes readiness evidence only and does not claim HISA certification, accreditation, or external approval is complete.

The broader Racing Operating System standardization model is documented in [Racing Operating System and Standardization Framework](../architecture/racing-operating-system-standardization-framework.md).
