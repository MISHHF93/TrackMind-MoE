# ISO/IEC 42001 AI Management Mapping

ISO/IEC 42001 is the AI management-system anchor for TrackMind Nexus. It guides how the platform documents AI accountability, intended and prohibited use, lifecycle controls, human oversight, evaluation evidence, monitoring, incident response, management review, and continual improvement.

This page describes readiness evidence and control intent. It does not claim that TrackMind, a deployment, a racetrack, or any AI model is ISO/IEC 42001 certified unless an independent certification process has been completed.

## AI Management Anchor

- Accountable AI ownership and oversight bodies.
- Intended-use and prohibited-use declarations for each AI capability.
- Risk classification, treatment, and human-governed protected-action rules.
- Model, prompt, feature, input, and dataset lineage.
- Evaluation evidence, approval records, rollback evidence, and change control.
- Monitoring for drift, low-quality input, unsafe recommendations, and policy blocks.
- Management review, audit evidence, corrective action, and continual improvement.

| Control objective | System evidence | Owner | Audit artifact |
|---|---|---|---|
| Establish governed, auditable racetrack AI operations. | RBAC, immutable audit logs, approval workflow, cited rulebook answers. | Compliance Officer | Policy exports, audit log hashes, approval records. |
| Protect safety-critical decisions from unapproved automation. | ResponsibleAIGovernor blocks regulated actions until approved. | Responsible AI Governor | Safety-rule test reports and approval records. |
