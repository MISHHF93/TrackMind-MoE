## Reusable racing evidence

ARCI rulebook, stewarding, custody, appeal, and local commission evidence should be packaged with HISA operational oversight categories and the universal evidence metadata used by the AI management system. The same evidence package can map to ARCI, HISA, local racing rules, ISO 42001, ISO 31000, SOC 2, PCI DSS, ISO 27001, and ISO 27701 when citations and control mappings identify how the evidence is reused.

This page models rule conformance readiness and evidence organization only; it does not claim ARCI approval or external certification is complete.
